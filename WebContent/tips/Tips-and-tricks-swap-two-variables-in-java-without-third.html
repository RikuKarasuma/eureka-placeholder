Tips and Tricks: How to swap two variables without using a third in Java.


I've always heard that this is a popular programming question to ask during an interview, it stuns veteran and junior programmers alike at first thought. I think it is because as problem solvers, we tend to think only on how to solve the problem at hand as fast and efficiently as possible instead of thinking about the tools we are using.

Now, focusing on tooling alot is not necessarily a good thing either since you are paid to solve the problems presented and tooling can be a real timesink. Instead it is important to find middle ground in knowing how to solve your problem domain and in knowning the capabilities of your choice tools, this will leave you more flexible and help deter scope pitfalls due to inexperience.
